How to use your phone as a camera

🔌

USB Cable Method

  1. Connect your phone to PC via USB data cable.
  2. On Android: go to Settings → Connected Devices → USB and select USB Tethering or File Transfer.
  3. On iPhone: trust the computer when prompted.
  4. Some phones appear as a UVC (webcam) device automatically — click Scan Cameras to find it.
📱

Use a Webcam App

  1. Install DroidCam (Android) or Camo (iPhone) on your phone.
  2. Install the matching desktop client on your PC.
  3. Connect via USB or Wi-Fi — your phone appears as a virtual webcam.
  4. Click Scan Cameras here and select the virtual device.
🌐

Open on Phone Directly

  1. Open this same page on your phone browser.
  2. Press Start Camera — your phone's camera streams right here.
  3. Works great for quick tests without any extra setup.

PhoneLens uses the browser's MediaDevices API — no data leaves your device.
